So, you are cetainly here because you searched for "Top 5 Free Tools" that you can use in everyday life to boost your productivity and organize your daily to-do tasks. Here, I have listed the Top 5 Tools that I myself have been using for a year. And yes they are free to use.

1. VS Code

Fast, extensible, and perfect for daily coding. All your programming languages in one editor. All you need is an extension for each language.

Available for: macOS, Linux, and Windows

2. Notion

Note-taking, planning, tracking my coding goals. Ideal for organizing and planning tasks.

Available for: Windows and macOS desktops, and iOS and Android mobile devices

3. Obsidian

Markdown-based knowledge base and idea writing. Ideal for note-taking.

Available for: Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android

4. GitHub Desktop

Push, pull, and version control without touching the terminal. Free for hosting websites as well as organizing your projects.

Available for: macOS, Windows, Android and iOS

5. Trello

Project boards to organize blog posts and personal tasks.

Available for: macOS, Windows, Android and iOS
